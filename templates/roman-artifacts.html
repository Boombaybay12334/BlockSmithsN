Roman Glass bowl

Details: This intricately blown glass bowl features a beautiful iridescent finish, a testament to the advanced glass-making techniques of the time.
Origin: Roman Empire (1st century AD).
History: Roman glassware was highly prized and often used in domestic settings, showcasing the wealth and sophistication of Roman society.
